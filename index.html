Our Donate Plan

We inspire our community to give so that everyone in child welfare is empowered to create their own success. You can help them take back their childhood and give them the confidence they need to succeed in life.

Shelter for a family

OUR PROJECTS

DELIVERING EYE HEALTH PROJECTS WHERE THEY ARE MOST NEEDED.

Sight For All is focused on collaborative eye health projects in key communities and countries. Our Projects are based on four fundamental humanitarian strategies – collaborative research, infrastructure support, sustainable education and the promotion of eye health.

Our Visionary teams have been or are currently involved in delivering eye health programs in Australia, Bangladesh, Bhutan, Cambodia, Lao, Mongolia, Myanmar, Nepal, Sri Lanka and Vietnam.
